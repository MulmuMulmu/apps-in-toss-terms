1. Purpose

These Terms of Service govern the use of Mulmumulmu, a mini app that helps users register food ingredients, review receipt OCR results, manage expected expiration dates, receive recipe recommendations, and share eligible food ingredients with nearby users for free.

2. Service Description

  • Receipt OCR and food item extraction.
  • Manual ingredient registration and ingredient inventory management.
  • Expected expiration date guidance based on ingredient information and purchase date.
  • Recipe recommendation based on user ingredients and recipe ingredient matching.
  • Free local sharing posts and user-to-user chat for arranging pickup.
  • Administrative review of reports, inappropriate posts, and OCR result quality.

3. Account and Login

Users may access the service through Apps in Toss login. The service may store an internal user identifier and an Apps in Toss user key to provide account-based features such as inventory, sharing posts, chat, reports, and OCR review logs.

4. Free Food Sharing Rules

Mulmumulmu supports free user-to-user sharing only. Users must not sell, exchange for money, demand compensation, or post items that are unsafe or restricted by law.

Prohibited items

  • Spoiled, contaminated, expired, or visibly unsafe food.
  • Opened food where safety, storage condition, or contamination risk cannot be reasonably checked.
  • Alcohol, tobacco, medicine, health functional food, or any regulated product.
  • Food requiring special temperature control when safe storage cannot be confirmed.
  • Items that include misleading descriptions, hidden payment requests, or illegal content.

5. User Responsibility for Shared Items

The posting user is responsible for accurately describing the item name, quantity, condition, storage method, purchase or expiration information, pickup place, and any known risk. The receiving user is responsible for checking the actual item condition, allergens, package status, storage condition, and suitability before accepting or consuming the item.

Mulmumulmu provides tools for posting, discovering, and arranging free sharing between users. The company is not the seller, buyer, owner, manufacturer, distributor, or inspector of user-posted items. The company does not guarantee the safety, freshness, legality, quality, suitability, pickup success, or truthfulness of user-posted shared items.

To the maximum extent permitted by applicable law, the company is not liable for disputes, damages, allergic reactions, food safety incidents, missed pickup, inaccurate user statements, or other issues arising from user-to-user sharing, unless caused by the company's intentional misconduct or gross negligence. The company may restrict posts, accounts, or chats and may cooperate with authorities when required to protect users or comply with law.

7. OCR, Expiration Date, and Recipe Recommendation

OCR, expiration date guidance, category classification, normalization, and recipe recommendation are assistive features. OCR may misread receipt text, and recommendation results may be incomplete or inaccurate. Users must review and correct the recognized item name, category, quantity, purchase date, expiration information, and allergens before relying on the result.

8. Reports and Administrative Review

The company may review reports, sharing posts, OCR results, ingredient statistics, and related logs to operate the service, improve quality, respond to user inquiries, prevent abuse, and remove unsafe or inappropriate content.

9. Suspension and Removal

The company may hide or remove posts, restrict sharing, suspend accounts, or delete content if a user violates these terms, posts unsafe items, requests payment, abuses chat, infringes another person's rights, or disrupts service operation.
